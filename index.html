🦆 HOW TO PLAY

```

🕹️ MOVEMENT

👆
Move left / right — push the joystick left or right to waddle. Moving fast into an enemy deals melee damage.

⬆️ JUMP

🦆
Hold joystick UP for 0.8s — a white charge bar fills above your duck. When full it fires automatically and you leap into the air. You must be on the ground for it to work.

🛡️ SHIELD

🔵
Hold joystick DOWN for 0.8s — the charge bar fills. When full, a glowing bubble appears around your duck blocking all incoming damage. Release DOWN to drop the shield.

⚡ SPECIAL ABILITY

✨
Release joystick back to centre — when the ABILITY bar under your HP is full and glowing, returning the joystick to neutral fires your special. Each weapon has a unique special. The bar refills every 3 seconds.

🎁 BOOSTS

Glowing orbs spawn on platforms throughout the match. Walk into one to collect it!
💚
HEAL
Instantly restores 30% of your maximum health. Great for turning the tide mid-fight.
❤️
HP UP
Permanently increases your maximum health by 25% — and scales your current HP up too.
⚡
DAMAGE BOOST
All your attacks deal 15% more damage for 8 seconds. A yellow ⚡ aura pulses around your duck while active. Stacks if you collect multiple!

💀 LIVES & WINNING

🩸
Each duck starts with 3 lives per round. Lose all HP or fall off the stage to lose a life — a 3-second respawn beam brings you back. Win 2 rounds to win the match!
```
